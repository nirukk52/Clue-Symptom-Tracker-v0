## Why They Fail

### Problem 1: Too Complex

Most apps try to track everything, resulting in:

- 5+ minute check-ins
- Form fatigue
- Abandoned after 2 weeks

**Our answer:** 20-second check-ins, flare mode, smart defaults

### Problem 2: No Insights

Raw data without meaning:

- "You logged 47 entries this month"
- No pattern recognition
- Users left to analyze themselves

**Our answer:** Evidence-backed insights with receipts

### Problem 3: Doctor-Useless Output

Data dumps that doctors ignore:

- Spreadsheet exports
- Too much information
- No summary or highlights

**Our answer:** Doctor-designed summaries that scan in 30 seconds

### Problem 4: Bad Day Blindspot

Same interface whether you're thriving or barely functional:

- Complex forms on flare days
- No accommodation for brain fog
- "Tracking is a luxury" mindset
